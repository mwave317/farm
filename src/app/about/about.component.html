My Homestead

So why did I decide to homestead?

I wanted to know where my food was coming from, as well as what they were eating. I belive that the animals should be treated right, raised out on pasture and given a great life.

This small family operation strives to provide the best quality meat year after year. Our cows and chickens are spend their lives on the pasture. They receive no antibotics, and no GMO's.
